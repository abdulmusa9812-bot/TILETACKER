Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Place tiles to merge matching numbers and keep the board alive. Plan ahead to chain merges and reach higher scores.

How do leaderboards work?

We use Apple Game Center for leaderboards. Make sure you’re signed in to Game Center to submit and view scores.

Why do I see ads?

Ads let us keep Tile Tacker free. We aim to balance monetization with a smooth gameplay experience.

Can I remove ads?

We’re exploring an ad-free option in a future update.

Privacy

Tile Tacker uses Apple frameworks and third-party advertising (Google AdMob). We do not collect personal information directly.
